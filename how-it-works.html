Create a Wallet or Register Your Account

The user can create an account by using a solid sign-up form or by linking a crypto wallet. BEX is friendly to global access, is anonymous, and does not involve individual financial integrations.

Activate the AI Autonomous Trading Engine

Once registered, users can activate the AI Autonomous Trading Engine, choosing from multiple trading cycles with daily return ranges of 0.8% to 1.5%. The engine immediately begins analysing market data using Bayesian logic and compounding principles from the Bayes–Euler framework.

Select Your Trading Plan and Cycle.

BEX has several trading cycles to suit varying risk and yield preferences. Users place crypto, create a plan, and leave the system to trade automatically. No third-party API keys, no brokerage accounts, no trading, or human intervention.

AI Engine Trades Internally

The autonomous engine implements strategies at the internal level by:
• Scalping • Arbitrage • Trend adaptation. All the actions are risk-filtered and optimized towards consistent daily growth.

Verify Trades On-Chain

Every transaction generated within the system is reflected on blockchain explorers, and users can independently verify.”
• Time-stamped trade activity • Transparent performance • Tamper-proof history. This ensures full accountability through on-chain proof-of-trade

Earn, Withdraw, or Compound

Users will be allowed to withdraw profits at the end of every cycle or compound them to gain faster profits. Our internal structure allows flexibility in balance management and complete control of earnings.

Referral Earnings and Unlock NFTs.

As users grow their assets, they automatically unlock NFT achievement tiers. Alongside this, the 5-level referral system (10%, 4%, 2%, 1%, 1%) provides ongoing rewards for expanding the BEX community.
